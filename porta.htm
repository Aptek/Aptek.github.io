Our 'Port A Room' Portable Buildings are purpose built Rooms. They are fully insulated, and all come with 2 double-sliding windows and 1 full-size door, all barred for security.

A standard unit measures 2.45m wide, by 6.0m long, the same base size as a standard 20' shipping container - making them very convenient to relocate & move.

Each Port A Room comes with 2 fluorescent lights, wall plugs, switches, and an RCD mains box. All doors have top of the line secure Key-Locks, with each unit coming with 2 keys for convenience.

Stackable

Every Port A Room is assembled in our yard by our expert team, then Wired & Certified by a registered Electrician. The Port A Room has an external caravan plug for easy power access.
