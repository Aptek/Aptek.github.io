Driving Directions to Aptek Assembly & Sales Yard

Heading North:

Take the 'Bombay' Pukekohe Exit off SH1 and turn Left.
Follow 'Mill Rd' / 'Pukekohe East Road' until you enter Pukekohe. Continue straight ahead.
Veer left at the Fork opposite 'Fruit World' and turn Left at Roundabout.
Follow 'Manukau Rd' to number '214' opposite 'Mitre 10'.


The Aptek Sales/Assembly Yard is adjoined to the 'Kumho Tyres' building. Head inside to see one of our team, who will gladly take you for a tour around the Yard and answer any questions you may have.
We can also provide on-the-spot answers for Finance Queries.
